This is The Colour Toy (yes, that's with a "u", but but Google should be able to find it anyway,) a tool for mixing and matching colours.

You can make colours using RGB or HSL sliders, and find new shades using automatic colour schemes.

Save nice colors (I threw in a US spelling there just in case) into a palette and bookmark it or share it with others.

Yes, this text is here mainly to make The Colour Toy slightly less search-engine-hostile. Please feel free to help out by linking to it everywhere you can.

And don't worry - this text will automatically dissappear the next time you visit.
